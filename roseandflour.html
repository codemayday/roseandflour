rose crepe cake with ganache and strawberry rose pastry cream

i still have memories of the house i lived in when i was young and naive and enchanted by the beauty of roses. at that age my vision was still tinted with disney princess movies and happily ever afters where roses grew rampant. there were a couple of rose bushes at the bottom of my long drive way and seeing them felt a little like dreamland dribbling into reality. my first attempt at checking their realness was rudely confirmed with a thorn in my thumb and hours of young me reduced to a blubbering mess in my bathroom begging my parents not to touch it. i kept my distance for a bit but soon enough i was drawn in again. bees to nectar, may to roses.
